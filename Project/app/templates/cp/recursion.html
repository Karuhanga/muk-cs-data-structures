A little literature...

Recursion in computer science is a method where the solution to a problem depends on solutions to smaller instances of the same problem (as opposed to iteration). The approach can be applied to many types of problems, and recursion is one of the central ideas of computer science.

Main Applications

The concept has wide application in computer science, including being used to deine data structures, like trees and in implementing divide and conquer style agorithms like merge sort.

Advantages

Can easily be applied to an arbitrary size of data. Recursice algorithms are also often very elegant compared to their iterartive counter parts.

Drawbacks

Poor implementation of recursion may lead to issues like infinite recuresion and multiple calculation of the same result needed in multpile calls.
